Our Signature Cocktails

The Gentleman's Manhattan

A timeless classic, The Gentleman's Manhattan combines the warmth of whiskey with the depth of sweet vermouth, and a touch of bitters to create a smooth and sophisticated cocktail. Served straight up or on the rocks, it's garnished with a maraschino cherry to add a hint of sweetness.

  • 2 oz whiskey
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Maraschino cherry for garnish

The Negroni

The Negroni is a bold and audacious choice for the connoisseur who appreciates bittersweet complexities. With its assertive blend of gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth, the Negroni commands attention and respect. Served over ice and garnished with an orange peel, this crimson-hued elixir offers a tantalizing aroma and lingering flavor.

  • 1 oz gin
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz sweet vermouth
  • Orange peel for garnish

The Classic Martini

The Classic Martini embodies elegance and refinement. This iconic cocktail is a simple yet sophisticated blend of gin or vodka and dry vermouth, served ice-cold and garnished with a lemon twist or olives. Whether you prefer it shaken or stirred, the Classic Martini is a must-try for every cocktail enthusiast.

  • 2 1/2 oz gin or vodka
  • 1/2 oz dry vermouth
  • Lemon twist or olives for garnish
