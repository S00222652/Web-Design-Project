About Us

The Sipping Society is a modern and sophisticated cocktail bar that has been serving the community since 2015. Our goal is to provide our customers with a unique and memorable experience, offering a wide variety of expertly crafted cocktails using only the finest ingredients.

Our talented team of mixologists is passionate about creating innovative and delicious drinks that cater to all tastes. From timeless classics to modern concoctions, our diverse menu offers something for everyone. We also host a variety of events, including mixology classes, guest bartender nights, and live jazz performances.

Located on the Sligo University campus, The Sipping Society is the perfect place to unwind after a long day of classes or enjoy a night out with friends. We invite you to join us for an unforgettable experience at our upscale establishment.
